Case Study

Artisan Coffee Co.

Industry E-commerce / Retail
Service Tier Autonomous
Timeline 5 weeks
Launch Date January 2026

The Challenge

Artisan Coffee Co. was struggling with an outdated e-commerce platform that wasn't converting visitors. Their organic traffic had plateaued, and they lacked the technical resources to implement proper SEO, performance optimization, or conversion tracking. They needed a modern, fast, and intelligent site that would not only showcase their premium coffee products but also continuously improve itself.

Our Agent-First Solution

We built a completely custom e-commerce platform with integrated autonomous agents that handle SEO, performance, and conversion optimization 24/7. The site features a clean, warm design that reflects the brand's artisanal values while leveraging cutting-edge agent technology to drive growth.

Agents Deployed

Autonomous SEO Agent

Continuously analyzes search trends, optimizes meta tags, generates schema markup, and suggests content improvements. Increased organic impressions by 45% in 3 months.

Performance Agent

Monitors Core Web Vitals, auto-optimizes images, implements lazy loading, and maintains 95+ Lighthouse scores across all pages.

Conversion Agent

Runs continuous A/B tests on CTAs, product page layouts, and checkout flows. Identified a 28% lift in add-to-cart rate through iterative testing.

Inventory Insight Agent

Tracks product performance, identifies bestsellers, and provides automated recommendations for product highlights and promotions.

Results & Impact

Within three months of launch, Artisan Coffee Co. saw dramatic improvements across all key metrics. The autonomous agents continued to optimize the site even during the team's sleep, delivering compounding returns.
